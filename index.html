The Problem

Low Engagement & Drop-off

What We Hear

Learners start strong but disengage quickly, whether it's an employee skipping training modules, a student abandoning a course, or a user churning from your platform.

How We Solve It

We design learning experiences that keep people hooked using interactive elements, micro-learning strategies, and progress mechanics that drive completion.

The Problem

Content That Doesn't Stick

What We Hear

People finish your training or course, but they don't retain what they learned. Information goes in one ear and out the other.

How We Solve It

We apply learning science principles—spaced repetition, retrieval practice, real-world application—to design experiences that create lasting behavior change.

The Problem

Best People Buried in Busywork

What We Hear

Your best people spend half their week on enrollment follow-ups, tuition reconciliation, campaign reporting, and CRM hygiene. The work that actually requires their judgment, the student conversations, the strategic calls, gets squeezed into whatever time is left.

How We Solve It

We embed a forward-deployed engineer with your team to build agents inside the systems you already use, across enrollment, finance, marketing, and CRM. The point isn't to replace your staff. It's to give them leverage so they can stay focused on what they do best: the students, the partnerships, and the decisions that actually move the program forward.
