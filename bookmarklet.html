Decision bookmarklet (Synthesia test)

This puts a small Send your decision panel on top of a Synthesia review page, without leaving Synthesia. It runs in your own logged-in tab.

Send Decision (iDesign)

Drag this button up to your bookmarks bar.

  1. Drag the button above onto your browser's bookmarks bar.
  2. Open your Synthesia review link and sign in as usual; leave your comments on the scenes as normal.
  3. When you're done, click the Send Decision (iDesign) bookmark. A panel appears in the top-right.
  4. Enter your name, choose Approved / Approved with changes / Changes requested and re-review, add an optional note, and it sends the decision to the team.
Test note: this is experimental. Synthesia allows it today, but if they change their security settings it could stop working — which is why the standalone decision page is the reliable fallback. Try it and tell us what happens.
